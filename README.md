# Eric Ekman — Data Science Portfolio

I am an MS Data Science student at American University and an experienced public-health researcher with hands-on experience across the full research lifecycle, including grant writing, IRB protocols, data collection, quality control, analysis, reporting, and final deliverables.

My interests include applied data science, public health analytics, mental health research, natural language processing, and interpretable machine learning. I am especially interested in projects that use data science to support health, mental health, policy, and socially meaningful decision-making.
